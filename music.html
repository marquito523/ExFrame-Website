ExFrame Music Commands

ExFrame Music Feature

    YouTube | Spotify | And Many Other are supported!

    Music

    Moderation Commands Fun Commands Music Commands Other Commands

    Music Commands:



    Play
    Use: m!play + [Music URL / Music Title]
    Does: Plays or adds to the queue a music requested.
    Requires: You must be in a music channel.

    Queue
    Use: m!queue
    Does: Shows next music that are going to play.
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    Shuffle
    Use: m!shuffle
    Does: Shuffles the current song playing
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    Skip
    Use: m!skip
    Does: skips the current song
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    Stop
    Use: m!stop
    Does: stops the music and ends the queue
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    Pause
    Use: m!pause
    Does: pauses the current song playing
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel, and the music needs to be playing.

    resume
    Use: m!resume
    Does: resumes the current song playing
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel and the music must be paused.

    np
    Use: m!np
    Does: Shows the current song playing, the length of the song, and other information on it.
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    clear-queue
    Use: m!clear-queue
    Does: clears the current queue.
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel.

    Debug
    Use: m!debug
    Does: Debugs the queue if there was an error.
    Requires: An error is required in order to use this command.

    Volume
    Use: m!volume
    Does: Changes the volume of all the songs in the queue, to what is requested.
    Requires: A music must be playing, and you must be in the music channel, requested volume needs to be between [1-300]

    ExFrame Music is a very used feature, and updated a lot to keep it up to date!
