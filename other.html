    >Other Commands:



    Suggestion
    Use: m!suggest + suggestion
    Does: Will suggest something to the owner, or admins.
    Requires: Nothing

    Invite
    Use: m!invite
    Does: Will send you an invite for the bot.
    Requires: Nothing

    Buy
    Use: m!buy + article + amount
    Does: Will purchase the article from the ExFrame Store you want.
    Requires: Enough Balance for the article

    Report
    Use: m!report + report/bug
    Does: Will send a report to marquito523.
    Requires: To not abuse this.

    Info User
    Use: m!memberinfo + @member
    Does: Will give you info on the user.
    Requires: Nothing

    Partners
    Use: m!Partners
    Does: Will send you an invite to partner bots.
    Requires: Nothing

    Bot info
    Use: m!bot info
    Does: Will give you the bot stats.
    Requires: Nothing

    Article List
    Use: m!article list
    Does: Will give you the articles you can buy from the ExFrame Store
    Requires: SOON


    You can find any kind of moderation commands in Other Section.
